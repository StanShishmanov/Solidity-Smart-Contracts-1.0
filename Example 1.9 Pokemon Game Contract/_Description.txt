Pokemon Game
Create a Pokemon game, that:
 - Knows ~10 different types of Pokemons
 - Any player has multiple Pokemons
 - Any player can say that he caught a Pokemon, but maximum once per 15 seconds (personalized timer)
 - The contract can list the Pokemons that a player has caught
 - The contract can list which player possess a Pokemon of certain type
 - Watch out for duplicates!
